Welcome to the Fossil Facility,

The purpose of this website is to inform the general public about the many different types of fossils their are and how to identify them. Most of the information this website will provide falls into one of these three kingdoms:

Kingdom Protozoa Information on single celled organisms.
Kingdom Animalia Infomation on multicellular heterotrophs.
Kingdom Plantae Information on multicellular autotrophs.
